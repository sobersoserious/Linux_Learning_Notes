#### 上层音频播放指令：

   > gst-launch-1.0 filesrc location=/data/ao_48K_16bit_MONO_30s.wav  ! wavparse ! audioconvert ! audioresample ! autoaudiosink

   > gst-launch-1.0 -e -v pulsesrc ! 'audio/x-raw,format=S16LE,rate=48000,channels=4,channel-mask=(bitmask)0x0000000000000033' ! audioconvert ! wavenc ! filesink location=/data/rec.wav
